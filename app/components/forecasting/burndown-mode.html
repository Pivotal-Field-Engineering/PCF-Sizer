Burndown

BurnDown Mode allows you to select a method for Shekel to predict a GB/Hour price to reach a target Burndown Date.

Selecting BurnDown: will set your Burndown Goal in Months = Forecast Duration, and will adjust Profit Margin in tandem with Plan Rolling Revenue Forecast to Recover Costs.

Selecting Month: will allow you to set the target month you wish to recover costs (Burndown Goal), and will adjust Profit Margin in tandem with Plan Rolling Revenue Forecast to Profit &/or Loss after the Burndown Goal Month has been reached.
